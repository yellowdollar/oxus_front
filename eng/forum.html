    About Forum
    Mining and Metallurgical Forum of Tajikistan is the first leading event in the field of mining and metallurgy in the Republic. At the platform for exchanging professional experience, industry experts will discuss the prospects for the development of the country's mining and metallurgical market, innovations in equipment and technologies for the extraction and processing of minerals, technologies for sustainable development and a number of other issues. The event is designed to increase the investment potential of the industry and ensure the establishment of effective cooperation between representatives of specialized companies.
