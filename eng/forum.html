    About Forum
    The International Jewellery Forum and Exhibition of Tajikistan, held under the auspices of the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan, is a significant major event in the history of independent Tajikistan in this area.

    The goal of the Forum is to create conditions for the popularization of the original Tajik traditions of jewellery art, its promotion to the international level and preservation as an intangible cultural heritage of the Tajik people, by promoting an open dialogue, exchange of experience and establishing direct contacts between various participants in different formats, attracting the investment community to cooperation, as well as strengthening interaction with international partners along the entire global chain of added value in the jewellery sector.

    The expediency of holding the Forum is justified by the tasks set by the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan for the breakthrough development of modern jewelry production and art of Tajikistan, with an emphasis on the historical perspective and reliance on the centuries-old traditions of national jewelry.

    The Tajik people have been famous for their jewelry art for thousands of years. This is confirmed by the jewelry collections in the world's leading museums, such as the British Museum and the Victoria and Albert Museum in London, where the ancient "Oxus Treasures" are kept, the Russian State Hermitage Museum, the Russian Ethnographic Museum in St. Petersburg, the Metropolitan Museum in New York and others.

    The extraction of precious and semi-precious metals and stones in Tajikistan today accounts for 30 percent of the mining industry, which is one of the country's priority industries.

    Forum Objectives
    • To present the achievements of the jewelry sector, to highlight the interaction of various companies along the entire global value chain of jewelry creation and production.
    • To present the world's leading manufacturers of jewelry equipment for the purpose of their possible interaction with jewelry manufacturers.
    • To identify potential areas of cooperation between companies involved in production along the entire global value chain, as well as in the promotion and sale of jewelry.
    • To create a platform for international cooperation by establishing contacts between local and foreign jewelry houses/brands/enterprises, including mining ones.
    • To create conditions for the presentation of works of local and foreign jewelry houses/enterprises/brands, as well as works of individual jewelry designers.
    • To promote the increase of the level of knowledge and skills in the field of jewelry art and its history, organization of jewelry business, design and marketing among local and regional jewelry manufacturers, designers, marketers, artisans.
    • To promote the exchange of experience between local manufacturers on the sustainable development of the jewelry industry of the republic using environmentally friendly materials and equipment.


    Forum Format
    • Plenary session and sessions. The opening of the Forum will be held with the participation of a representative of the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan and international partners/participants
    • Exhibition of jewelry equipment
    • Exhibition - fair of jewelry products
    • Fashion show in collaboration with jewelry brands
    • Visit to jewelry clusters in Dushanbe
    • Visit to museums and cultural complexes of the country
    • Educational sessions and master classes

    Forum Participants
    • Representatives of the public sector involved in the production of equipment for the jewelry sector and the production of jewelry products
    • Representatives of business circles and the private sector (companies, manufacturers of precious and semi-precious metals and stones, jewelry products, jewelry houses and brands, artisan jewelers, jewelry designers, investors)
    • The diplomatic corps accredited in the Republic of Tajikistan
    • Experts and researchers in the field of jewelry art, collectors
    • Representatives of educational institutions and cultural organizations, students of the Institute of Fine Arts and Design, as well as other educational institutions studying jewelry specialties
    The Forum will involve about 50 local jewelry enterprises, individual artisans and jewelry designers, as well as more than 200 foreign participants representing jewelry brands, individual artisans creating jewelry, designers from Central Asian countries and other countries (Azerbaijan, Armenia, Georgia, India, Iran, China, the United Arab Emirates, Pakistan, Russia, Turkey, other EU and Middle East countries).

    Expected results
    • Strengthening interaction with international partners along the entire global value chain in the jewelry sector
    • Establishing contacts between companies operating in mining and jewelry production, as well as their sustainable development
    • Exchange of experience between participants in the use of innovations and new technologies in the jewelry sector
    • Increasing knowledge and experience, strengthening the professional potential of local jewelry producers, artisans, young masters, designers
