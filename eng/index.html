    Participant registration of the International Jewelry Forum and Exhibition of Tajikistan

    International Jewelry Forum and Exhibition of Tajikistan will be held on March 24-26, 2025 in Dushanbe. We invite heads of line ministries and agencies, financial institutions, business communities, and other esteemed parties to participate in this Forum. Official delegations, regional and international companies, financial institutions, experts and other stakeholders wishing to participate in the Forum are invited to register on the website www.jewelryforum.tj by 1 March 2025.
    International Jewelry Forum «At the origins of the Oxus treasure»
    March 24-26, 2025 | Dushanbe, Tajikistan
    One of the most significant events in the jewelry industry, the International Jewelry Forum and Exhibition will open in Dushanbe from March 24 to 26, 2025.
    The event is held under the auspices of the Government of the Republic of Tajikistan and brings together craftsmen, designers, manufacturers, investors and experts from more than 15 countries. The forum creates a unique platform for popularizing Tajik traditions of jewelry art, exchanging experiences and strengthening international cooperation. The event will include plenary sessions, educational sessions, a jewelry fair, a fashion show and visits to cultural sites. Join the main jewelry event of the year and become part of history, where ancient traditions meet innovations!
    Venue: Hotel "Hyatt Regency Dushanbe" and International Exhibition Center of Dushanbe "Dushanbe EXPO Centre".
